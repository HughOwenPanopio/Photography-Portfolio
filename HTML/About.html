About Hugh Owen Panopio

Born in Cauayan City, Isabela, Philippines. Hugh first picked up a camera at age of nineteen - influenced by nature and street photography. After graduating college he made the move to Manila, where he naturally felt the transition to event photography.

Hugh enjoys creating timeless and emotive imagery. He is inspired by his favorite photographers such as Lara Jade, Julia Trotti, Taylor Jackson and Matt Granger, just to name a few.

He currently resides in his hometown and regularly travels for assignments, working between Luzon, Visayas and Mindanao.
